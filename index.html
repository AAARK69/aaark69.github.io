01. Mission Overview

Introduction

This project analyzes the aerodynamics, telemetry data, and optimization profiles required for next-generation atmospheric and sub-orbital flight trajectories.

Core Objectives

  • Determine optimal launch parameters using fluid dynamic modeling.
  • Analyze the structural integrity of localized payload designs.
  • Map real-world data points to predictive mathematical functions.

Hypothesis

By optimizing surface geometry coefficients, structural drag can be systematically mitigated to increase fuel mass efficiency parameters by a calculated margin.
